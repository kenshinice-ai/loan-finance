Questions

Common lending questions.

Do you work with first-home buyers?

Yes. The advice process includes deposit planning, borrowing capacity, government-scheme checks, and approval timing.

Can you help if my bank has already declined me?

Often, yes. A decline can be caused by policy fit rather than affordability. We review the reason before recommending next steps.

Is the consultation obligation-free?

The initial conversation is obligation-free and focused on whether there is a suitable path forward.

What documents do I need to apply?

Typically ID, recent payslips, bank statements, and details of existing debts. We provide a full checklist after your first call.

How quickly can we settle?

Most applications move from assessment to conditional approval within 48 hours. Settlement timing depends on the property and lender.
